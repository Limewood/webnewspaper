Submission guidelines

When submitting an article, please follow the guidelines below:

  • Use proper grammar and language as well as you can. Inappropriate language in articles might lead to the article being deleted.
  • Images in articles should be no larger than 150kB. An image is not required.
  • Articles containing slander or personal attacks are not allowed and will be deleted
  • Articles containing incorrect information may be deleted if it is found that proper investigations and analysis of the information has not been carried out before submission or the posting of incorrect information was intentional.
  • Articles containing copyrighted material will be deleted if the author does not hold the copyright. Good sources for public domain images can be found here.
